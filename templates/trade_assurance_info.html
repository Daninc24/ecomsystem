Frequently Asked Questions

Is Trade Assurance free?

Yes! Trade Assurance is completely free and automatically applied to all orders. There are no additional fees or charges.

How long do I have to file a claim?

You have 30 days from the delivery date to file a Trade Assurance claim. After this period, claims cannot be processed.

What happens to my payment during a claim?

Your payment is held securely until the claim is resolved. If the claim is approved, you'll receive a full refund. If rejected, payment is released to the vendor.

How quickly are claims processed?

Most claims are reviewed and resolved within 24-48 hours. Complex cases may take up to 7 business days for thorough investigation.
