üìè How to Measure

Chest/Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest/bust, keeping the tape measure level.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, which is the narrowest part of your torso.

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, about 8 inches below your waist.

Foot Length

Stand on a piece of paper and mark the longest toe and heel. Measure the distance between marks.
