How do I track my order?

You can track your order in several ways:

  • Log into your account and visit the "Orders" page
  • Use our Track Order tool with your order number
  • Check the tracking email sent to your registered email address
How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location and shipping method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day
Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on orders over ${{ store_config.shipping_info.free_shipping_threshold }}. Express and overnight shipping rates apply for faster delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items:

  • Items must be in original condition with tags attached
  • Original packaging and accessories must be included
  • Some items like personalized products are not returnable

Learn more about returns

How do I start a return?

Starting a return is easy:

  1. Log into your account and go to "Orders"
  2. Find the order and click "Return Items"
  3. Select the items you want to return and reason
  4. Print the prepaid return label
  5. Package and ship the items back to us
When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned items. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
