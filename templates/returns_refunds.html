Frequently Asked Questions

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive your returned items. The refund will appear on your original payment method.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Currently, we don't offer direct exchanges. Please return the item for a refund and place a new order for the item you want.

What if my item arrives damaged?

If your item arrives damaged, please contact our customer service immediately. We'll provide a prepaid return label and process your refund or replacement quickly.

Can I return items bought with a discount or coupon?

Yes, you can return items purchased with discounts or coupons. The refund amount will be the actual amount you paid after discounts.
